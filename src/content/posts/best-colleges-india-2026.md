## How to Choose the Right College

1. Check NIRF rankings
2. Look at placement records
3. Check fee structure
4. Visit the campus if possible
5. Talk to current students

Choosing the right college is one of the most important decisions of your life. Research well, visit campuses, and make an informed decision.
